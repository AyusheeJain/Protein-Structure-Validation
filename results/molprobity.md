# Why MolProbity?

MolProbity is one of the most widely used tools for validating experimentally determined protein structures.

It evaluates

- Steric clashes
- Ramachandran outliers
- Rotamer conformations
- Bond geometry
- Hydrogen placement

Instead of analyzing the raw experimental structure (4EYD), the corrected structure (4EYDH) generated after MolProbity optimization was used for downstream structural analysis.

This improves the reliability of hydrogen bond detection, electrostatic calculations, and residue interaction analysis.
