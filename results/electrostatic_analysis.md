# Electrostatic Surface Analysis

Electrostatic potential was calculated in UCSF ChimeraX using the Coulombic electrostatic method.

Surface coloring:

- Red → negatively charged regions
- White → neutral regions
- Blue → positively charged regions

Electrostatic mapping helps identify

- Potential ligand-binding regions
- Protein-protein interaction interfaces
- Charge distribution
- Surface polarity

The Coulombic method estimates electrostatic potential directly from atomic partial charges without requiring an external APBS calculation.
